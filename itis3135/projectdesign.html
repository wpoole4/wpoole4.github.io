Project Design

This is the plan and deisgn for Will P's project for ITIS3135.

Project Overview

  1. This application is used for advertisement and a catalogue of Nona Poole's Poshmark listings of jewelry and clothing. While the selling is done via Poshmark, the website will contain extra information and show previously sold items and more
  2. The intended users are people interested in buying clothes, jewelry, and more from Nona and want to see more information about the items she sells and her credibility as a seller.
  3. The website will contain a home page, a page for new Poshmark listings, a page for previously sold items, a page about Nona herself, and possibly some more.
